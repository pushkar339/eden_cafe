Our Story

BORN FROM
A SINGLE CUP

Eden Cafe began in 2012 with a simple obsession — finding the perfect cup of coffee. What started as a tiny corner roastery has grown into a beloved urban sanctuary, where every bean is chosen with intention and every brew is a ritual.

We source our beans directly from small-scale farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and India — building relationships with growers who share our belief that quality begins at the root.

Our kitchen follows the same philosophy. Every item on our menu is made fresh, in-house, using seasonal produce and time-honoured techniques.

In-House Roasting

OUR
ROASTING

We roast in small batches, every week, to ensure every cup you drink is as fresh as it gets. Our master roaster brings decades of experience and a stubborn refusal to compromise on flavour.

  • Small-batch weekly roasts
  • Single-origin & blends
  • Direct trade from 6 countries
  • Light, medium & dark profiles

LIGHT ROAST

Fruity, floral & bright. Notes of citrus and berry.

MEDIUM ROAST

Balanced & smooth. Notes of caramel and hazelnut.
